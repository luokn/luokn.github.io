Transformer 中的注意力：∀k,q∈RDk\forall k,q \in \mathbb{R}^{D_k}∀k,q∈RDk​, K=(k1,k2,⋯ ,kN)K=(k_1,k_2,\cdots,k_N)K=(k1​,k2​,⋯,kN​)，Q=(q1,q2,⋯ ,qN)Q=(q_1,q_2,\cdots,q_N)Q=(q1​,q2​,⋯,qN​), a:RDk×RDk→R,k,q↦αa:\mathbb{R}^{D_k} \times \mathbb{R}^{D_k} \to \mathbb{R},k,q \mapsto \alphaa:RDk​×RDk​→R,k,q↦α， 令A=(aij)∈RN,αij=a(ki,qj)A=(a_{ij}) \in \mathbb{R}^{N},\alpha_{ij} = a(k_i, q_j)A=(aij​)∈RN,αij​=a(ki​,qj​)，则：

Attention(K,Q,V)=Softmax(A)VAttention(K,Q,V) = Softmax(A)VAttention(K,Q,V)=Softmax(A)V

aaa常用以下几种：

  • 点积：a(ki,qj)=kiqja(k_i, q_j) = k_i q_ja(ki​,qj​)=ki​qj​
  • 缩放点积：a(ki,qj)=kiqjDka(k_i, q_j) = \frac{k_i q_j}{\sqrt{D_k}}a(ki​,qj​)=Dk​​ki​qj​​
  • 双线性：a(ki,qj)=kiWqja(k_i,q_j)=k_i W q_ja(ki​,qj​)=ki​Wqj​
  • 拼接：a(ki,qj)=W[ki∣∣qj]a(k_i,q_j)=W[k_i||q_j]a(ki​,qj​)=W[ki​∣∣qj​]

核注意力：将点积、缩放点积、双线性、拼接可统一描述为键值在内积空间的映射以及其内积空间的内积运算，即：

a(ki,qj)=⟨ϕ(ki),φ(qj)⟩a(k_i,q_j) = \langle \phi(k_i) , \varphi(q_j) \ranglea(ki​,qj​)=⟨ϕ(ki​),φ(qj​)⟩

其中ϕ,φ:RDk→H\phi,\varphi:\mathbb{R}^{D_k} \to \mathcal{H}ϕ,φ:RDk​→H，H\mathcal{H}H为内积空间，则可令核函数为K=⟨,⟩\mathcal K = \langle, \rangleK=⟨,⟩，即：

K(ki,qj)=⟨ϕ(ki),φ(qj)⟩\mathcal K(k_i,q_j) = \langle \phi(k_i) , \varphi(q_j) \rangleK(ki​,qj​)=⟨ϕ(ki​),φ(qj​)⟩

对于点积模型，令ϕ=φ=id\phi=\varphi = \mathop{id}ϕ=φ=id，则有：

K(ki,qj)=⟨ki,qi⟩=kiqj\mathcal K(k_i,q_j) = \langle k_i , q_i \rangle= k_i q_jK(ki​,qj​)=⟨ki​,qi​⟩=ki​qj​

对于缩放点积模型，令ϕ(ki)=kiDk,φ=id\phi(k_i)=\frac{k_i}{\sqrt{D_k}}, \varphi = idϕ(ki​)=Dk​​ki​​,φ=id，则有：

K(ki,qj)=⟨kiDk,qi⟩=kiqjDk\mathcal K(k_i,q_j) = \langle \frac {k_i}{\sqrt{D_k}} , q_i \rangle = \frac{k_iq_j}{\sqrt{D_k}}K(ki​,qj​)=⟨Dk​​ki​​,qi​⟩=Dk​​ki​qj​​

对于双线性模型，令ϕ(ki)=kiW1,φ(qi)=W2qi,W=W1W2\phi(k_i) = k_i W_1, \varphi(q_i) = W_2q_i,W=W_1W_2ϕ(ki​)=ki​W1​,φ(qi​)=W2​qi​,W=W1​W2​，则有：

K(ki,qj)=⟨kiW1,W2qj⟩=kiW1W2qj=kiWqj\mathcal K(k_i,q_j) = \langle k_i W_1 , W_2q_j \rangle = k_i W_1 W_2 q_j = k_i W q_jK(ki​,qj​)=⟨ki​W1​,W2​qj​⟩=ki​W1​W2​qj​=ki​Wqj​

对于拼接模型，令：

ϕ((ki1,⋯ ,kiDk))=(u1ki1,⋯ ,uDkkiDk,1,⋯ ,1)∈R2Dk\phi((k_i^1,\cdots,k_i^{D_k}))=(u_1 k_i^1, \cdots , u_{D_k}k^{D_k}_i,1,\cdots,1) \in \mathbb{R}^{2 D_k}ϕ((ki1​,⋯,kiDk​​))=(u1​ki1​,⋯,uDk​​kiDk​​,1,⋯,1)∈R2Dk​ φ((qi1,⋯ ,qiDk))=(1,⋯ ,1,v1qj1,⋯ ,vDkqjDk)∈R2Dk\varphi((q_i^1,\cdots,q_i^{D_k}))=(1, \cdots, 1, v_1 q_j^1, \cdots ,v_{D_k}q^{D_k}_j) \in \mathbb{R}^{2 D_k}φ((qi1​,⋯,qiDk​​))=(1,⋯,1,v1​qj1​,⋯,vDk​​qjDk​​)∈R2Dk​ W=(u1,⋯ ,uDk,v1,⋯ ,vDk)∈R2DkW=(u_1,\cdots,u_{D_k},v_1,\cdots,v_{D_k}) \in \mathbb{R}^{2D_k}W=(u1​,⋯,uDk​​,v1​,⋯,vDk​​)∈R2Dk​

则有：

K(ki,qj)=⟨(u1ki1,⋯ ,uDkkDki,1,⋯ ,1),(1,⋯ ,1,v1qj1,⋯ ,vDkqjDk)⟩=urkir+vsqjs=W[ki∣∣qj]\begin{aligned} \mathcal{K}(k_i, q_j) &= \langle (u_1 k_i^1, \cdots , u_{D_k}k^{D_k}i,1,\cdots,1) , (1, \cdots, 1, v_1 q_j^1, \cdots ,v{D_k}q^{D_k}_j) \rangle = u_r k_i^{r} + v_s q_j^{s} = W[k_i||q_j] \end{aligned}K(ki​,qj​)​=⟨(u1​ki1​,⋯,uDk​​kDk​i,1,⋯,1),(1,⋯,1,v1​qj1​,⋯,vDk​qjDk​​)⟩=ur​kir​+vs​qjs​=W[ki​∣∣qj​]​
