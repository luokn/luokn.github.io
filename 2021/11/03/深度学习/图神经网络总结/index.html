$$
x_i^\prime = \phi \left( x_i, \mathop{aggregate}{j \in \mathcal{N(i)}} \varphi (x_i,x_j,\theta{ij}) \right)
$$

其中为激活函数，为融合方法，为激活函数

GraphSAGEConv

$$
\hat x_i = \mathop{aggregate}{j \in \mathcal{N}(i) \cup {i}}(\theta x_j)
\newline
x^\prime_i = \frac{\hat x_i}{|| \hat x_i ||{2}}
$$

  • 其中为神经网络，比如 MLP

DNAConv

$$
x_v^{(t)} = h_\theta^{(t)} \left( x^{(t)}{v \leftarrow v}, {x^{(t)}{v \leftarrow w} | w \in \mathcal{N}(v)} \right)
\newline
x^{(t)}_{v \leftarrow w} = \mathop{Attention} \left(
x_v^{(t-1)} \theta_Q^{(t)},
[x_w^1,\cdots,x_w^{(t-1)}] \theta_K^{(t)},
[x_w^1,\cdots,x_w^{(t-1)}] \theta_V^{(t)},
\right)
$$

  • 表示的映射矩阵，表示不训练的 GCNConv

  • 表示每个点的位置

x_i^\prime = \sigma \left( \sum_{r \in \mathcal{R}} \sum_{j \in \mathcal{N}^{r}{i}} \frac{1}{c{i,r}} W_r x_j + W_0 x_i \right)
$$
